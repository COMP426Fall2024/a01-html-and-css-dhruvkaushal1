Overview

Icarus, son of the master craftsman Daedalus, attempted to escape Crete using wings made of feathers and wax. Despite his father's warnings, Icarus flew too close to the sun, causing the wax to melt and sending him plummeting into the sea.

Character Role
Icarus The young man who flies too close to the sun.
Daedalus Inventor and father of Icarus, who creates the wings.
